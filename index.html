Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add a new medication?
Tap the "+" button on the home screen to add a new medication. Enter the name, dosage, frequency, and set reminders to stay on track.
Is my health data private?
Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. MindDose does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal health information with third parties.
How does mood tracking work?
After logging a dose, you can record how you're feeling. Over time, MindDose helps you spot patterns between your medication and mood through your journal entries.
Can I export my medication history?
Yes! MindDose lets you export your history as a PDF, making it easy to share with your doctor or keep for your own records.
What devices are supported?
MindDose is available for iPhone and iPad running iOS 17.0 or later.
